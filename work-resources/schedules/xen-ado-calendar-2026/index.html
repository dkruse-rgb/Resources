Source and method

How this calendar was built

The dates come from US South Region – Baton Rouge–Beaumont General Manager Notice No. 10, issued January 1, 2026, titled 2026 6&2 ADO Matrix.

This page maps each job number directly to the matching matrix turn number: XEN1B uses Turn 1, XEN2B uses Turn 2, and so on through XEN9B. Turn 9 follows the same listed date pattern as Turn 1 in the matrix. Verify the assignment-to-turn mapping against current local instructions before relying on the calendar.
