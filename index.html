About the project

A practical resource—not an official union publication.

This site is being assembled as an independent document library for railroad employees. It is designed to make work resources easier to find, understand, and share.

Documents should be checked against current agreements, official notices, and applicable law before being relied upon. The site can later be expanded with summaries, searchable indexes, location-specific pages, and direct downloads.
